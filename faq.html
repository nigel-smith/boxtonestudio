Recording & Voiceover

It's a purpose-built, acoustically treated home studio in Ware, Hertfordshire. Equipment includes a Shure SM7B (the industry standard for voiceover and vocals), Lewitt LCT 440 Pure large-diaphragm condenser, a matched pair of Lewitt LCT 040 small-diaphragm condensers for stereo and acoustic work, and a Focusrite Saffire Pro 40 interface. Everything is run through Logic Pro on an Apple M4 Mac Mini.

For location work, a portable rig built around the RME Babyface Pro FS is available.

The studio is suited to commercial, corporate, audiobook, podcast, and e-learning voiceover content. Sessions are recorded in the acoustically treated studio and delivered as broadcast-ready files or raw stems — whichever works best for your production workflow.

Voiceover recording starts from £40 per hour.

Yes. Musician demo recording is available from £150 and is particularly well suited to singer-songwriters, auditions, and social content. Guitar, vocals, and acoustic instruments can all be captured with professional microphones and mixed in Logic Pro.

It's also worth knowing that the engineer is an active gigging musician with 35 years of experience — so the session is shaped by someone who understands how music should sound, not just how to operate a DAW.

Yes — podcast recording, editing, and post-production is available from £25 per hour. Sessions are delivered clean and consistent, ready to publish. Whether you need a one-off recording or ongoing production support, get in touch to discuss what would suit you.

Finished files are delivered within 5 working days of the session unless a different timeline has been agreed in advance. A backup copy is retained for 30 days following delivery.
