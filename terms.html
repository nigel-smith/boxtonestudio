Booking Terms & Cancellation Policy

These terms apply to all services offered by BoxTone Studio, including guitar tuition, recording sessions, PA hire, and guitar services. By making a booking you agree to the terms below. Last updated: May 2026.

Guitar Tuition

Booking
Lessons are available on a recurring weekly basis or as individual sessions. Slots are reserved on a first-come, first-served basis and are not confirmed until acknowledged by BoxTone Studio.

Payment
Payment is due at the time of each lesson unless a monthly block booking arrangement has been agreed in advance. Accepted payment methods are listed on the booking site.

Cancellation by the student

Notice given Charge
More than 48 hours before the lesson No charge — lesson rescheduled or refunded
24–48 hours before the lesson 50% of the lesson fee
Less than 24 hours or no-show Full lesson fee payable
Please note: Repeated late cancellations or no-shows may result in the slot being offered to another student.

Cancellation by BoxTone Studio
In the unlikely event that a lesson needs to be cancelled by BoxTone Studio, you will be given as much notice as possible and the lesson will be rescheduled at no extra cost, or a full refund issued if rescheduling is not possible.

Illness
If a student is unwell, please give as much notice as possible. Cancellations due to genuine illness will be treated sympathetically — wherever possible lessons will be rescheduled rather than charged.

Recording & Voiceover Sessions

Booking & deposit
Recording and voiceover sessions require a 50% non-refundable deposit to secure the booking. The remaining balance is due on the day of the session before work begins.

Cancellation by the client

Notice given Charge
More than 7 days before the session Deposit forfeited — no further charge
2–7 days before the session 50% of total session fee
Less than 48 hours or no-show Full session fee payable

Session overruns
Sessions are booked by the hour. Time overrunning the booked session length will be charged at the standard hourly rate, subject to availability.

Delivered files
Finished files will be delivered within 5 working days of the session unless otherwise agreed. BoxTone Studio retains a backup copy for 30 days following delivery.

PA Hire with Engineer

Booking & deposit
PA hire bookings require a 50% non-refundable deposit to secure the date. The remaining balance is due no later than 7 days before the event, or on the day for bookings made within 7 days of the event.

Cancellation by the client

Notice given Charge
More than 14 days before the event Deposit forfeited — no further charge
7–14 days before the event 75% of total booking fee
Less than 7 days before the event Full booking fee payable

Travel & access
The client is responsible for ensuring safe and adequate access for load-in and load-out of equipment. Any parking costs, congestion charges, or access fees not disclosed at the time of booking will be recharged to the client.

Damage to equipment
BoxTone Studio equipment is covered by insurance. However, any damage caused by wilful misuse or negligence on the part of the client or their guests may be recharged at the cost of repair or replacement.

Guitar Services

Estimates & quotes
Where possible a fixed price will be agreed before work begins. For complex repairs, an estimate will be provided. If additional issues are discovered during the work, you will be contacted before any additional charges are incurred.

Turnaround
Most setup and repair work is completed within 5 working days. An estimated completion date will be provided at the time of drop-off.

Collection
Instruments should be collected within 14 days of completion. BoxTone Studio cannot accept liability for instruments left beyond this period.

Liability
BoxTone Studio takes every care with instruments in its possession. Liability is limited to the cost of the work carried out. Customers are advised to ensure their instruments are covered by their own insurance whilst in transit.

General

Privacy
Contact details provided at the time of booking are used solely for the purpose of managing your booking and will not be shared with third parties.

Changes to these terms
BoxTone Studio reserves the right to update these terms at any time. The current version will always be available at this page. Existing bookings are governed by the terms in place at the time of booking.

Questions
If you have any questions about these terms, please get in touch via boxtonestudio.co.uk before making a booking.
